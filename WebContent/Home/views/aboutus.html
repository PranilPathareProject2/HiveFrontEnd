About NIIT

Welcome to NIIT, your number one source for all types courses. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of education, with a focus on three characteristics: dependability, quality and uniqueness.

When Pranil first started out, his passion for spreading education drove him to quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming career.
