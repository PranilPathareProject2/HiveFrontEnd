Privacy Policy

NIIT Hive is committed to safeguarding your privacy online. We recognize your need for reasonable control of personally identifiable information that you share with NIIT Hive.

When you join us, we need to know your name and e-mail address. This will be used only for the service you have sought and would not be under any circumstances passed on for any other commercial purpose. NIIT Hive will not disclose any of your personally identifiable information except when we have your permission or under special circumstances, such as when we believe in good faith that the law requires it.

Although we track the total number of visitors to each of sites in an aggregate form to allow us to update and improve our sites, personally identifiable information is not extracted in this process. Any cookie technology (where our servers deposit special codes on a visitor's computer) will only be used to track non-personal information such as type of browser,operating system, and domain names to improve our visitor's online experience.
